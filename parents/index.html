What happens next

How requests are handled

  1. You receive an email confirmation with a ticket number.
  2. The school’s compliance contact is notified.
  3. The school verifies your relationship to the student. The simplest method is a one-time code your student’s teacher can issue through the school’s normal parent-communications channel.
  4. Verified requests are approved, denied, or escalated. Every step is recorded.
  5. You receive a response by email. Data exports and deletions are completed by a school administrator.
