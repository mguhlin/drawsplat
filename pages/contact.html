What happens next

How requests are handled

  1. Your request is written to the project’s Google Sheet (the same data store every other DrawSplatTM compliance form uses).
  2. The configured compliance admin gets an email notification.
  3. A real person reads it and replies at the email you provided.
  4. If you asked for a quote on Support & Professional Learning, you’ll get a scope outline plus pricing options.
