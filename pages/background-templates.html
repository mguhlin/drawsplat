Open Whiteboard

Panel Background Templates

Education backgrounds for lessons, stations, reflection, and visual thinking.

These original DrawSplatTM SVG backgrounds are free, open-source classroom templates. Use them as locked panel backgrounds so students can work on top of the structure without moving or deleting it.

Original DrawSplatTM templates. No third-party wallpaper assets are bundled.

Use in DrawSplatTM

Choose Open full-size in DrawSplatTM to launch the whiteboard with that background applied to the active panel.

Full-size panels

Templates open as the only content on a blank DrawSplatTM panel, scaled to fill the workspace so students can write on top.

Teacher Layer

These work well with assignment mode because the background is part of the panel, not an editable student object.
