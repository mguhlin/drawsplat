Why $33?

Many visual collaboration tools use recurring subscriptions. DrawSplatTM is smaller, static-first, and offered as-is, so a low one-time price keeps it accessible while still giving individual users a way to support the work.

What “as-is” means

The one-time license covers the current version as provided. It does not include hosted storage, guaranteed support, custom development, compliance review, or future upgrades unless those are offered separately.

For campuses or districts

The $77 campus license covers one self-hosted school site. The $199 district license covers a district deployment and includes both the MySQL backend option and the Google Apps Script version. Managed hosting, SSO setup, custom admin dashboards, privacy/legal review, or realtime multi-user backend development are separate services.
