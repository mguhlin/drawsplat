Why $49?

Many visual collaboration tools use recurring subscriptions. DrawSplat is smaller, static-first, and offered as-is, so a low one-time price keeps it accessible while still giving serious users a way to support the work.

What “as-is” means

The one-time license covers the current version as provided. It does not include hosted storage, guaranteed support, custom development, compliance review, or future upgrades unless those are offered separately.

For schools or districts

The $499 self-hosted site license can cover a school or district using the current static app as-is. It does not include managed hosting, SSO setup, custom admin dashboards, privacy/legal review, or realtime multi-user backend development unless those services are added separately by agreement.
